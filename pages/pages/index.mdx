⚠️ Pages functionality is experimental

You can place entry files in the `pages` directory. 


File extensions such as `.md`, `.mdx`, `.jsx`, `.tsx` are supported for entry files.
In a `.mdx` file, you can place your JSX components along with the markdown content.


/pages
- ── _app.jsx
- ── _template.html
- ── [index.mdx](./)
- ── [pageA.mdx](./pageA)
- ── sub
    - ── [index.mdx](./sub/)
    - ── [pageB.mdx](./sub/pageB)
